Tips for Healthy Eating:

  • Choose whole, unprocessed foods whenever possible.
  • Include a variety of fruits and vegetables in your diet.
  • Limit added sugars and unhealthy fats.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water throughout the day.
  • Practice mindful eating and savor each bite.
  • Plan your meals ahead of time to avoid unhealthy choices.
  • Consider portion sizes to maintain a balanced diet.
  • Experiment with herbs and spices to enhance flavors without adding extra salt.
